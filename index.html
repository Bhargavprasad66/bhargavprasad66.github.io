Olist E-Commerce Analytics — Multi-Tool BI

End-to-end analysis of the Olist Brazilian e-commerce dataset spanning customers, orders, payments, reviews, products, sellers, and geolocation. Modeled and cleaned the full relational dataset, then built three parallel BI deliverables on top of it: a KPI-driven Excel dashboard, an interactive Tableau workbook, and a Power BI report.
